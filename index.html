Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptile that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin colours is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most Chameleons have prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting facts about pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly more complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
